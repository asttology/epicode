Focaccia with caramelized onions,

cranberries,and brie

wow you taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart
cranberries,and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally vegas

Featured in:

Fresh Cranberries Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia

  • 4 cups(500g) all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoon instant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoon salt
  • 13/4(350g)lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil,plus more for the pan

For the topping

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil,for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie,cut into cubes(scrape rind of with a paring knife if desired,tough it's not
    necessary)
  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

1. make the focaccia dough

In a medium-sized mixing bowl,or in a salad mixer with a paddel attachment,mix togheter all

the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed.You don't need to actually knead the

dough,tough I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate

the las of the flour...
